# War

A classic card game brought to life on iOS. Play against the CPU in fast-paced rounds where only the highest card wins!

## How to Play

War is simple but addictive:

1. **Tap the Deal Button** — Press the button to deal cards for both you and the CPU
2. **Compare Cards** — Your card appears on the left, the CPU's card on the right
3. **Win Points** — Whoever has the higher card wins the round and scores a point
4. **Keep Playing** — Play endless rounds and try to beat the CPU's score!

**Card Values:** Cards range from 2 to 14 (following standard playing card values). May the highest card win!

## Installation

### Requirements
- iOS 14 or later
- Xcode 13.0 or later (for developers)

### Getting Started

1. **Clone or download** this repository to your Mac:
   ```
   git clone <repository-url>
   cd War
   ```

2. **Open in Xcode:**
   ```
   open War.xcodeproj
   ```

3. **Run the app:**
   - **On Simulator:** Select a simulator in Xcode and press `Cmd + R`
   - **On Device:** Connect your iPhone, select it as the target, and press `Cmd + R`

4. **Start playing!** Tap the button and begin your battle against the CPU.
